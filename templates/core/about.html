Have Questions?

Building and expanding wafer, solar cell, and module manufacturing facilities demands high investment. Success relies on strong infrastructure, advanced processes, and long-term financial commitment.

How do solar panels work?

Solar panels capture sunlight and convert it into electricity using photovoltaic (PV) cells.

What are the main types of solar systems?

The two main types are solar PV systems (electricity) and solar thermal systems (heating).

What are the benefits of solar energy?

Solar energy is renewable, cost-saving, low-maintenance, and eco-friendly.

How much space do solar panels need?

A typical 1kW solar system needs 100 sq. ft. of shadow-free space.
